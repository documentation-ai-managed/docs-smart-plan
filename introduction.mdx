## Overview

Splan provides an ecosystem of services that automates sales and marketing for real estate developers. You streamline lead generation, client nurturing, and deal closures with integrated tools tailored for the property sector. Optimize your business processes and boost conversion rates using innovative automation solutions.

<Callout kind="info">
Splan integrates seamlessly with your existing CRM and property management systems.
</Callout>

## Quick Start

Get up and running with Splan in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Create your account at `https://dashboard.example.com` and verify your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get API Key" icon="key">
    Navigate to settings and generate your `{API_KEY}`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integrate" icon="code">
    <CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,Python">
      ```javascript
      const splan = new SplanClient({ apiKey: 'YOUR_API_KEY' });
      const leads = await splan.leads.list();
      console.log(leads);
      ```
      ```python
      from splan import SplanClient
      client = SplanClient(api_key='YOUR_API_KEY')
      leads = client.leads.list()
      print(leads)
      ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Campaign" icon="rocket">
    Set up your first automated campaign and monitor results.
  </Step>
</Steps>
